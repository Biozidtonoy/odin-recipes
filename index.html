Kacchi Biryani:

Kacchi biriyani is a beloved dish from the Indian subcontinent, particularly associated with the culinary traditions of Hyderabad, Lucknow, and parts of Pakistan. Unlike the more common Pakki Biryani, where cooked meat is layered with rice, Kacchi Biryani is made by layering raw marinated meat with partially cooked rice and then cooking it together, usually by a slow, dum cooking method. This gives the dish its signature tenderness, flavor, and rich aroma.

Beef curry:

Beef curry is a flavorful and hearty dish enjoyed in various cuisines across the world, particularly in South Asia, the Caribbean, and parts of Africa. It typically involves tender chunks of beef cooked in a rich, spiced gravy, and it can be made in various styles depending on regional preferences, cooking methods,and spice blends.
